About My Grandmother





My name is Sarah Dahan, I am 87 years old and born in 1933 in the village of Skura, Morocco.
I was born as the smallest sister in a family of 6 children.
I knew Judah, my husband, at the age of 13. He grew up in the same village where I grew up.
We got married after a few weeks and at 15 I already gave birth to my eldest daughter, Sima.
I have 7 children, all of whom I gave birth to in Morocco, including Kobi,
We did to him "brit mila" in Casablanca just before we boarded the plane,
Only Ruth, my little daughter, was born in Israel.
We immigrated to Israel in 1964, to Jerusalem. For 25 years I worked at Shaare Zedek Hospital. All the doctors at the hospital today know me and remember me from my time there.
My hobbies are to cook, knit and embroider. Now in the Corona period I have closed the club I go to 3 times a week so I mostly do it at home.
I am most eagerly waiting for the near future to end the plague and I can go back out to the neighborhood garden in the Kiryat Yuval neighborhood of Jerusalem, to meet all my friends there and also to come back Go to the club I go to.

Q&A

1. The Three main diffrences between web1,web2 and web3

  1. web1 is read-only. Displays superficial data. There is no interaction between people, users are used to passively receive information.
    In web2 it is writable, allows interaction between users and sites and among themselves.
    web3 is excutable ie running dynamic applications, here it starts to get information processing and AI integration.

  2. In web1 crawlers cannot be used and web2 and web3 have the option to use.

  3. In web1 an internet search can be performed on one specific address. web2 has the option of searching with defined tags.
    In web3, the option to go beyond searching in web2 was also added using semantic search.
    Additionally in web1 there is no refreshment at all. In web2 there is a small amount of refreshes and in web3 there is the greatest amount of refreshes.

2. Medicines side effects Use Case

    Let's take an example of a side effect of a specific drug- we take the query: "acamol side effects"
  1. UC Name: Example For web1:
    Primary actor: User
    Main scenario:
    1. Write the specific site that talks about the side effects of acamol.
    2. Click 'Search'.
    Result: site specific
  2. UC Name: Example For web2:
    Primary actor: User
    Main scenario:
    1. Type in the search box: "Acmol Side Effects"
    2. Click 'Search'.
    Result: Links to web pages with the tags of the words in the query.
  3. UC Name: Example For web3:
    Primary actor: User
    Main scenario:
    1. Type in the search box: "Acmol Side Effects"
    2. Click 'Search'.
    Results: Links to web pages that are also semantically related (in addition to what is said in web2) to the query.
